Popular Destination

Premier Destinations

Mexico City & the Heart of a Civilization

A sprawling metropolis layered with epochs Aztec foundations at Templo Mayor, colonial plazas like Zócalo, Baroque-era palaces and world-class museums. Wander through lively neighborhoods such as Coyoacán and Condesa.

Mexico

Yucatán Peninsula & Chichén Itzá

Walk amidst sunlit ruins, where the stepped stones of Chichén Itzá rise dramatically, echoing the ingenuity of the Maya. Close by, plunge into cenotes natural sinkholes offering crystalline blue waters.

Baja California Sur – Where Desert Meets the Sea

Mexico's natural contrasts meet in cinematic harmony: arid desert landscapes curve down to cobalt pacific waters. The beaches of Cabo San Lucas offer world-class resorts and dramatic arches.

Cancún & Riviera Maya

From the sparkling white sands of Cancún to the quiet luxury of Tulum, this region unfolds as a sun-soaked tapestry. Dive among coral reefs, snorkel over tropical fish, enjoy lively nightlife.

Jalisco & the Soul of Mexico

Guadalajara is alive with mariachi music and tequila heritage. Visit the town of Tequila to see fields of agave stretching green until the horizon. In nearby towns artisanal crafts shine.

Oaxaca & Colonial Wonders

The historic centre of Oaxaca City invites visitors into coral-hued courtyards, artisan markets, and mezcal tastings. Nearby, Monte Albán perches above the valley as a testament to Zapotec brilliance.

Copper Canyon & Northern Highlands

Picture canyon vistas more dramatic than Colorado's, weaving through sierra valleys by train, remote pueblos, and trails to explore the sky-high forests of Chihuahua.
